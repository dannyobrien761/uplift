what's an uplift ? and why do it ?

Bringing bikes and people up a hill using a purpose built 16 holder MTB uplift trailer and a 16 seater mini bus.
The goal is to connect more trails and mountain bikers in Slieve Bloom. The uplift route will connect Offaly and Laois trails and give mountain bikers more downhills, more trails and more fun per session.
